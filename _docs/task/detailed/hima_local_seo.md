- [ ]  Provide a “Real” Address. Make sure it can be consistent everywhere.
- [ ]  Use a Local Phone Number.
- [ ]  Cull Duplicate or Wrong GBP Listings.
- [ ]  Choose the Best Primary Category.
- [ ]  Add Secondary Categories (Up to 9).
- [ ]  Complete Every Section of Your Profile.
- [ ]  List Your Products and Services.
- [ ]  Add Plenty of High Quality Photos (and Videos).
