# UMKM Tax Workflow

## Intake before calculating

Record the tax period, taxpayer type (individual/entity), business activity, turnover basis, VAT status, location, relevant invoices/withholding evidence, and whether the question is educational or will be used for a filing/payment decision. Do not infer legal eligibility from turnover alone.

## Calculation response

1. State the tax concept being discussed and the assumptions.
2. Show the formula, inputs, unit, and arithmetic in a table.
3. Separate tax due, tax already withheld/paid, cash needed, and any unknown amount.
4. List documents and records the user should retain.
5. Provide a verification checklist before filing or paying.

## Compliance checklist

- Verify the taxpayer identity, classification, and current registration status.
- Verify the relevant period, transaction date, and whether revenue is gross or net of returns/discounts.
- Verify the current rate, threshold, eligibility, filing method, payment code, and deadline from an official Directorate General of Taxes source.
- Reconcile invoices, sales records, bank receipts, withholding slips, and prior payments.
- Keep the calculation and source evidence with the filing records.

## Boundaries

Do not present a tax rule, threshold, deadline, or eligibility condition as current unless checked against an official source. Flag cross-border transactions, VAT treatment, audits/disputes, restructuring, substantial transactions, or conflicting records for a licensed Indonesian tax professional.
