# Meta Ads Campaign Playbook

## Select the response mode

- Use **quick response** for a narrow question: answer directly in 3–5 useful points; do not invent a full campaign structure.
- Use **planning mode** for a new campaign, a rebuild, a scaling plan, or a performance diagnosis: use the structure below.

## Planning-mode workflow

1. Confirm product, conversion event, AOV, gross margin, variable fulfilment cost, target market, pixel/CAPI status, daily budget, current CPA/ROAS, and audience size.
2. Calculate break-even CAC before setting a performance target: `break-even CAC = gross profit per first order − allowable acquisition overhead`. If repeat purchases matter, state the retention assumption separately.
3. Split the plan by audience temperature:
   - Cold: prospecting and creative/audience learning.
   - Warm: video viewers, engagers, and site visitors with a relevant proof or objection angle.
   - Hot: cart/checkout or equivalent high-intent visitors, excluding purchasers.
4. Test one primary variable at a time: creative angle first, then offer/landing page, then audience. Keep the conversion event, attribution setting, and measurement period explicit.
5. Make scale/hold/pause decisions only after enough conversion signal for the account. Avoid fixed universal thresholds; use the expected CPA and conversion volume for that product.

## Required plan sections

1. Business economics and target CPA
2. Campaign/ad-set/ad structure with audience exclusions
3. Budget allocation and learning objective
4. Creative matrix: audience pain, angle, proof, visual hook, CTA
5. Measurement plan: spend, CPM, CTR, CVR, CPA, purchase value, MER when available
6. Decision rules: what to keep, iterate, pause, or scale and why
7. Risks: tracking, stock, landing-page mismatch, fatigue, policy claims

## Metric definitions

| Metric | Formula | Use |
|---|---|---|
| CTR | Link clicks / impressions | Diagnose creative/message relevance |
| CVR | Purchases / landing-page sessions | Diagnose offer, page, checkout, or traffic quality |
| CPA | Spend / purchases | Compare with break-even CAC |
| ROAS | Purchase value / spend | Revenue efficiency; not profitability by itself |
| MER | Total revenue / total marketing spend | Blended business impact |

## Creative test card

For every concept, state: `Audience → pain/desire → angle → first-frame hook → proof → offer → CTA → hypothesis → success metric`. Avoid claims that cannot be substantiated and check current platform policy before launch.
