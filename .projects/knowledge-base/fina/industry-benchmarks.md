# Industry Benchmarks

## F&B (Food & Beverage)

### Cost Structure
| Cost Category | Typical % of Revenue |
|---------------|---------------------|
| Food Cost | 30% - 35% |
| Beverage Cost | 20% - 25% |
| Labor Cost | 20% - 25% |
| Rent | 10% - 15% |
| Utilities | 3% - 5% |
| Marketing | 2% - 4% |
| Others | 5% - 10% |
| **Target Net Profit** | **10% - 15%** |

### Key Ratios
- **Gross Margin:** 65% - 70%
- **Inventory Turnover:** 8 - 12 times/year
- **Break-even Sales:** 60% - 70% of capacity

### Warning Signs
- Food cost > 40% of revenue
- Labor cost > 30% of revenue
- Rent > 20% of revenue
- Gross margin < 60%

## General Financial Health Benchmarks

### Healthy Ranges (All Industries)

| Metric | Healthy Range |
|--------|---------------|
| **Current Ratio** | 1.5 - 3.0 |
| **Quick Ratio** | 1.0 - 2.0 |
| **Debt-to-Equity** | 0.3 - 1.0 |
| **Gross Margin** | 40% - 70% |
| **Net Profit Margin** | 10% - 20% |
| **ROA** | 5% - 15% |
| **ROE** | 15% - 25% |
| **Cash Coverage** | 3 - 6 months |

### Early Warning Indicators

- Negative operating cash flow for > 3 months
- Current ratio < 1.0
- Debt-to-equity > 2.0
- Net profit negative for > 6 months
- Gross margin declining > 20% YoY
- Cash balance < 2 months expenses

---

## Notes

- Benchmarks dapat bervariasi tergantung ukuran, lokasi, dan model bisnis
- Bandingkan dengan bisnis serupa di area yang sama untuk akurasi
- Startup early-stage mungkin memiliki profit margin yang lebih rendah
- Konsultasikan dengan ahli keuangan untuk bisnis dengan karakteristik unik
